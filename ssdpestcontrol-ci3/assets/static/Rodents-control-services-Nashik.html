Rodents Control Services in Nashik

SSD Pest Control - House Mouse, Roof rats, Norway rat, Bandicoot. Rodents are very clever mammals. Their breeding rate is also high as one pair can multiply to 800 in a year. They infest, destroy, contaminate more than they feed, because of their continue “GNOWING ACTION”. Rodent cause severe diseases like rat bite fever, Laptospirosis, Plague, Trichinosis, Salmonellosis and also food poisoning..

Rats cause a lot of damage. As the teeth of rodents continue to grow over their lifetime, they need to gnaw on hard substances such as lead and plastic pipes, insulation material and electric wiring. This habit increases the risk of short circuits and fires. In addition to this, they transmit a number of dangerous diseases such as Salmonella, Leptospirosis (Weil's disease), Tuberculosis and even tape worms..

Rodents Pest Covers

01

House Mouse Rodents

They are small in size very clever, good climbers and live in homes & social environments.

02

Roof Rodents

They are large in size, their tail is longer than their head+body length, sexual maturity attended in 8 to 12 weeks. Body weighs up to 150-200gms.

03

Bandicoot Rodents

Very ferocious looking rodent with long face. It does considerable damage to fields & buildings. Adult weight is between 300 to 1000gm.

04

Norway Rodents

Commonly found in drainage. Their tail is short than their head+body length. They prefer the food containing carbohydrates & proteins. Body weighs up to 400gm.

Treatment for Rodent Control ?

The SSD Pest Control uses three treatment modalities to relieve the rats,Sound environmental sanitation is the most effective strategy. Rats required three things food, water & shelter. If these are denied rats will naturally decrease in density

Baiting Method

FAQ

1How do I know if I have a rat infestation ?

It is often easier to spot signs of a problem, rather than the actual pest. Rat droppings, scratching noises, footprints (running tracks), distinctive smell, rub marks, damage, nests and burrows.

2 Why are there rats in my house ?

Rats in your property doesn’t necessarily mean your place is dirty. Rats are attracted to anywhere there is food, water and shelter. Only small holes are required for a rat to squeeze through.

3How to get rid of rats without poison or traps ?

Other than traditional traps, there are bait stations with the bait, or rodenticide, that is used being safe to use even in areas where there are children and pets.

4What types of rat killers are there ?

Our qualified and professionally trained service technicians use a range of different rodenticides when controlling rats. Call us at for more information..

5 How do you get rid of rats in the house ?

There are common treatments you can undertake such as traps, but we strongly recommend a professional service due to the damage rats can cause to property, causing fires through chewing of cables and the potential to carry disease
